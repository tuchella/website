about

Short version

I make music, code music, compose music and listen to music

Slightly Longer Version

I'm a musician and coder from Switzerland. Exploring conflicts and synergies of human/computer relationships in fixed-media pieces, interactive sound installations and live coding performances. My work acts as a means to explore new ways of interacting with an ever expanding digital world. All while trying and failing to help both the general public and myself to understand how we can mold that world.
After a professional education in software development I'm currently completing a BA Sound Arts at Hochschule der Künste Bern.
